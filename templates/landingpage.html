Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the university matching?
Our AI matching algorithm has a 92% accuracy rate based on student outcomes. We analyze thousands of data points including academic performance, extracurriculars, and institutional preferences to provide the most accurate matches possible.
Is my personal information secure?
Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption to protect your data and never share your information with third parties without your explicit consent. Your privacy is our top priority.
Can I use EduPath for graduate school applications?
Currently, we specialize in undergraduate admissions, but we're actively developing graduate school tools. Sign up for our newsletter to be notified when this feature launches.
How often is the university data updated?
We update our database continuously throughout the year, with major updates each admissions cycle. Our team verifies all information directly with institutions to ensure accuracy.
